BatchMol - Credits & Licenses
============================================================

BatchMol is a specialized command-line tool for high-throughput 
analysis of quantum chemical data, including orbital phase 
stabilization, ESP mapping, and batch property exports.

This application is provided as open-source software. 
It bundles several third-party libraries, each governed 
by their own licenses:

SCIENTIFIC COMPUTING & QUANTUM CHEMISTRY:
------------------------------------------------------------
1. PySCF (Apache License 2.0)
   https://pyscf.org
   (Used for electronic structure calculations and GTO handling)

2. cclib (BSD 3-Clause License)
   https://github.io
   (Used for parsing quantum chemical log files)

3. NumPy (BSD License)
   https://numpy.org

4. SciPy (BSD License)
   https://scipy.org

VISUALIZATION & EXPORT:
------------------------------------------------------------
5. PyVista & VTK (MIT / BSD License)
   https://pyvista.org | https://vtk.org

6. Matplotlib (PSF/BSD compatible)
   https://matplotlib.org

UTILITIES:
------------------------------------------------------------
7. Click (BSD License)
   https://palletsprojects.com
   (Command-line interface provider)

8. tqdm (MIT/MPL License)
   https://github.io
   (Progress bar integration)

9. psutil (BSD License)
   https://github.com
